Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

DOTS ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Professional Information (Consultants): Skills, work experience, educational background, certifications, banking details for payouts
  • Purpose: Account creation, identity verification, profile display, payment processing

Location Data:

  • Foreground Location: Precise or approximate device location used to find nearby consultants, display services on maps, and enable address autocompletion
  • Background Location: Used for real-time tracking features such as consultant en-route tracking to appointments
  • Purpose: Location-based services, consultant matching, navigation, service optimization

User Content:

  • Messages, photos, files, and documents shared within the app
  • Purpose: Facilitate communication between users and deliver content to intended recipients

Payment Information:

  • We do not store credit card details. Payments are processed through secure third-party gateways (Yoco)
  • We store transaction IDs, payment statuses, and transaction history
  • Purpose: Process payments and maintain transaction records

Device Information:

  • Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, IP address
  • Purpose: App optimization, troubleshooting, security, and fraud prevention

Usage and Analytics Data:

  • App interactions, features used, screens viewed, session duration, click patterns
  • Purpose: Improve user experience, identify bugs, optimize performance, and develop new features

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing appointments and payments
  • Facilitating communication between clients and consultants
  • Personalizing user experience
  • Sending notifications about appointments, messages, and updates
  • Improving app functionality and developing new features
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Advertising ID

4. Permissions Required

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: Network communication, authentication, database access
  • Location (Fine/Coarse/Background): Find nearby consultants, maps, real-time tracking
  • Camera: Profile pictures, image sharing, video calls
  • Microphone: Voice messaging, audio/video calls
  • Storage: Cache maps, store sent/received files, save media
  • Push Notifications: Alerts for messages, appointments, updates
  • Vibration: Haptic feedback for notifications
  • Foreground Services: File uploads, maintaining call connections
  • Full Screen Intent: Display incoming call notifications even when device is locked
  • Advertising ID: Serve personalized advertisements

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Other Users: Profile information visible to users searching for consultants
  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Yoco), cloud storage, analytics providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Object to automated decision-making

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
